Context

In this challenge, we were asked to select a scenario from a pre-defined list, and design an application based on the scenario and the keywords. The scenario we selected is “Fashion Thing”.

What is upStyle?

upStyle is a sustainable thrift shopping service that allows its users to share styling inspirations and buy or rent secondhand items from user-styled outfits in a sustainable and affordable fashion. It aims to reduce the carbon footprint caused by the textile industry by connecting local stylists, users, and businesses. In doing so, it encourages users to recycle clothes rather than buy new. 

Target Audience

01.

Individuals who wish to get rid of their unwanted clothing pieces.

02.

Individuals who wish to get styling inspirations.

03.

Individuals who wish to buy/rent secondhand items.

Branding

Color Palette

We chose rust to be the primary colors. The rust color symbolizes a slower way of life, strong connection with Nature and more sustainable living. 

Font

We decided to use Lato and Montserrat as the main fonts. The semi-rounded details of the letters give Lato a feeling of warmth, while Montserrat creates a majestic yet fun look.
