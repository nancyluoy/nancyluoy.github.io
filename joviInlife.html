Context

Jovi Inlife is VIVO’s smart home solution that connects smart devices together. It allows users to easily and securely control various internet- or bluetooth-connected home devices -- ranging from light bulbs and plugs to washers, ACs, and more -- from anywhere.

Challenge

01.

How might we present the possibilities in controlling and monitering a smart home to users ?

02.

How might we build a steady, scalable and reasonable framework that is able to accommodate the future business growth ?

Design Goals

Recognition

Users can easily tell what the app is about and what they can do with it.

Usability

Offer features that are helpful and easy to use

Attachment

Build trust through our app offerings

Analysis

Journey Map

We worked with our user research team and did some research on current and potential users. We created a journey map along with their thoughts and actions- from when they enter the app to when they leave - to identify issues and find opportunities.
